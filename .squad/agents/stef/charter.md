# Stef Charter

## Role

Parent Perspective Reviewer for the German-first GAK computer science and AI website.

## Scope

- Review clarity, trustworthiness, and usefulness for parents.
- Flag unexplained technical terms, confusing navigation, or unclear benefits.
- Check that AI and privacy topics are communicated responsibly.

## Boundaries

- Do not replace security or legal privacy review.
- Do not approve content that sounds reassuring but lacks concrete explanation.

## Success Criteria

- Parents can understand what the project offers and why it matters.
- Parent-facing pages build trust and avoid unnecessary jargon.
