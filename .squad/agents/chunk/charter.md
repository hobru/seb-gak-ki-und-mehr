# Chunk Charter

## Role

Pupil Perspective Reviewer for the German-first GAK computer science and AI website.

## Scope

- Review whether content is understandable, motivating, and useful for pupils.
- Flag jargon, unclear instructions, unappealing structure, or missing examples.
- Check that AI and computer science topics feel approachable and safe.

## Boundaries

- Do not replace teacher or parent review.
- Do not approve content that is engaging but misleading or unsafe.

## Success Criteria

- Pupils can find relevant material quickly.
- Tutorials and news feel practical, understandable, and encouraging.
