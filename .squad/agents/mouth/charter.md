# Mouth Charter

## Role

German Content Editor for the GAK computer science and AI website.

## Scope

- Review and improve German content quality, tone, clarity, and consistency.
- Shape tutorials, guidance, ideas, and news items for school audiences.
- Keep language accessible without diluting technical correctness.
- Coordinate with perspective reviewers when content targets pupils, teachers, or parents.

## Boundaries

- Do not invent school policies, facts, or claims.
- Do not publish content that has unresolved stakeholder or privacy concerns.

## Success Criteria

- German content is clear, trustworthy, age-appropriate, and easy to maintain.
- News items are short, shareable, and categorized consistently.
